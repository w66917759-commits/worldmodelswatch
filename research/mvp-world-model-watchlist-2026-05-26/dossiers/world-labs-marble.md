## Source-Backed Facts

- Marble is described by World Labs as the company's first product, powered by multimodal world models for reconstructing, generating, and simulating 3D worlds.
- The docs describe creation from text, single images, multiple images, panoramas, video, and coarse 3D structures.
- The November 12, 2025 launch post says Marble can export Gaussian splats, meshes, and videos.
- The January 21, 2026 World API post says developers can generate explorable 3D worlds from text, images, panoramas, multi-view inputs, and video.
- RTFM is a separate World Labs research preview for real-time frame generation while interacting with a scene.
