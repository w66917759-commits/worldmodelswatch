## Source-Backed Facts

- Google DeepMind describes Genie 3 as a general-purpose world model for generating interactive environments.
- The Genie 3 post says it can generate worlds navigable in real time at 24 fps, with consistency for a few minutes at 720p.
- The model page describes real-time interaction at 20-24 fps, 720p output, consistency/stability, and Street View grounding in the Project Genie product framing.
- The January 2026 Google post says Project Genie is an experimental research prototype powered by Genie 3, Nano Banana Pro, and Gemini.
- Genie 1 has an arXiv paper: "Genie: Generative Interactive Environments."
- Genie 2 was announced by DeepMind as a foundation world model capable of action-controllable, playable 3D environments prompted by a single image.
